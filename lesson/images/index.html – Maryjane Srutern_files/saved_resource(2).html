Maryjane's Chicken Shawarma

Introduction

This type of shawarma is my signature recipes that is known by all my friends because I make this so often.but now i will be making it for public consumption It’s off the charts for effort vs output: just a handful of everyday spices, a splash of lemon, garlic and olive oil transforms into the most incredible flavour.

When this chicken is cooking, the smell is incredible. Incredible!!!

How to Serve Chicken Shawarma

There are two main ways to serve Chicken Shawarma.

  1. As Chicken Shawarma wraps – with tomato, lettuce, and a simple yoghurt sauce on the side.
  2. nd the other way is with rice and salads on the side. Thought I usually do serve pita bread or another type of flatbread as well, just because it’s very on theme.

Side Dishes

A few things that would be very much at home alongside Chicken Shawarma:

  • Rice Pilaf with Nuts and Dried Fruit
  • Chickpea Rice Pilaf
  • Lemon Rice Pilaf
